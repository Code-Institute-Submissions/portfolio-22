AWS Latest

06/05/2020

AWS and Slack have announced that they will be forming a multi-year agreement and will deliver new services for advanced collaboration. The core goal of AWS and Slack it seems from this new partnership is to enable development teams to communicate and collaborate as well remotely as they typically can when in the same room. Some comments from both sources have even indicated the possibility of managing AWS resources from within the Slack Client.
